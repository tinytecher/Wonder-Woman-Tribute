THE HISTORY OF WONDER WOMAN

Wonder Woman first appeared in 1941 and was created by psychologist and writer William Moulton. She was orinially crafted from clay and brought to life by her mother Queen Hipployta, would be raised on a tropical island and gifted superpowers by the greek gods. Wonder Woman was trained to be a powerful warrior, while also taught about love and peace. Along with her powers, such as strength, speed, wisdom and a hunters mind, Wonder woman was also given the Gauntlet of Atlas, to increase her strength, the Laso of Truth and was also gifted the ability to fly.
